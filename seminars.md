#### -Apr 3, '26 [Differntial Form and Integration on Riemann Surface](/assets/pdf/Differntial_Form_and_Integration_on_Riemann_Surface.pdf). 
This seminar, [Riemann Surfaces](https://sustech-topology.github.io/undergrad/), is held by [Yifei Zhu](https://yifeizhu.github.io/) at SUSTech in Spring 2026. This lecture briefly introduces the differential forms on a real manifold, the definition of De Rham Cohomology, holomorphic and meromorphic forms on a Riemann Surface, integration of 1-form, and free homotopy invariance. period homomorphism and Residue Theorem.
